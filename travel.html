Travel is therapeutic

Travel can have many benefits for humans, including: Stress reduction: Travel can help you de-stress by taking you away from your daily routine and into new surroundings. Improved mental health: Travel can help you feel calm and boost your mental health. Increased creativity: Travel can help you come up with new ideas and improve your problem-solving skills. Personal growth: Travel can help you understand different cultural perspectives and ways of viewing the world. Appreciation for diversity: Travel can help you learn to appreciate and celebrate similarities and differences. New perspectives: Travel can help you develop a new way to perceive life, who you are, and how you spend your time
